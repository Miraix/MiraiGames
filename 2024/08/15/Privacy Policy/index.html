Privacy Policy for MiraiGames

Introduction

Thank you for choosing MiraiGames. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information. Please read this policy carefully  
to understand your rights.

Information We Collect

When you use our games or services, we may collect the following information:

  • Basic information: Your username, password, email address, device model, and operating system version.
  • Game data: Your game progress, game settings, in-app purchase history, and friends list.
  • Device information: Your device identifier, IP address, and device location information (with your consent).
  • Other information: Information you provide when you contact us or participate in activities.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing game services: Creating your game account, managing your game data, and providing you with a personalized gaming experience.
  • Improving game experience: Analyzing your gaming behavior to optimize game features and enhance game quality.
  • Communicating with you: Sending game updates, event information, and customer support responses.
  • Marketing: With your consent, we may send you promotional materials for our games or services.
  • Compliance with laws: Complying with legal obligations and protecting our rights.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with third parties under the following circumstances:

  • **  
    Service providers:** We may share your information wi  
    th our partners or service providers to help us provide game services (e.g., cloud storage providers, payment processors).
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized acces  
s, use, or disclosure. These measures include but are not limited  
to:

  • Data encryption: Encrypting your sensitive information.
  • Access controls: Limiting access to personal information.
  • Security monitoring: Regularly monitoring our systems for security vulnerabilities.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information: You can view or request a copy of your personal information.
  • Correct your personal information: You can update or correct inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Delete your personal information: In certain circumstances, you can request that we delete your personal information.
  • Object to the processing of your personal information: You have the right to object to the processing of your  
    personal information for certain purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please review this policy periodically for updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at game@miraix.top.
